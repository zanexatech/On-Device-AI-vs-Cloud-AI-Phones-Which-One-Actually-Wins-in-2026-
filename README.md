## Key Comparison

| Feature        | On-Device AI                     | Cloud AI                          |
|---------------|---------------------------------|-----------------------------------|

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which is faster: on-device AI or cloud AI?
On-device AI is faster for small tasks because it avoids internet delay.

### Is on-device AI more secure?
Yes, since data stays on your device, it reduces exposure to external servers.

### Why is cloud AI still used?
Cloud AI provides much higher computing power and supports advanced AI models.

### Can AI work without internet?
Yes, on-device AI works offline for supported tasks.

### What is the future of AI?
The future is a hybrid model combining on-device speed with cloud power.
